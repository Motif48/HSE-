Our Story

Where Every
Bite Feels Like
Heaven

Heaven's Spices Eateries (HSE) was born from a deep love for authentic Kenyan flavours — the kind that warm your soul, tell a story, and bring people together around the table.

From our signature Ugali & Nyama Choma to spiced biryanis, crispy chapatis, and hand-crafted cocktails, every dish is made fresh daily with locally sourced ingredients and generations of culinary wisdom.

We are not just a restaurant — we are an experience. Come hungry, leave happy, return often.
